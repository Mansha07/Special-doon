Shikhar Falls

This is one of the famous picnic spot in Dehradun. Trekking through waterfalls and passing by shallow pool provides you genuine feel of nature many species of butterfly can be seen here and the water is very clear. To reach the waterfall you have to do trekking about 1km. You must wear good shoes. It is about 13 kilometer from Dehradun. Here you can find many species of birds which you never seen before. People use to come here for photography.
